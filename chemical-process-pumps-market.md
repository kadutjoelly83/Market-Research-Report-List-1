<p><p>Chemical process pumps are essential for transporting fluids in industrial applications. The market is categorized into two main types: monopole and double pole pumps. Monopole pumps have a single power source, providing a compact solution suitable for smaller, less demanding applications. In contrast, double pole pumps utilize two power sources, offering increased reliability and efficiency for more complex operations. Both types are vital in ensuring the safe and efficient handling of various chemicals in processing plants.</p></p>
