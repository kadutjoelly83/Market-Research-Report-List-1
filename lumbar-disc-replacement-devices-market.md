<p><p>Lumbar disc replacement devices are designed to alleviate back pain by replacing damaged intervertebral discs. The market is primarily segmented into two types: metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer devices. Metal-on-metal devices feature components made entirely of metal, offering durability and strength. In contrast, metal-on-biopolymer devices combine metal with biopolymer materials to enhance biocompatibility and reduce wear. Each type aims to restore spinal function while minimizing complications, catering to different patient needs and preferences in spinal surgery.</p></p>
