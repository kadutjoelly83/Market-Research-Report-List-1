<p><p>Surge suppressors are critical devices used to protect systems from pressure surges and fluctuations. The Switch Type activates automatically during pressure spikes, diverting excess energy. Pressure Limiting Type maintains a preset pressure level, ensuring systems operate within safe limits. Flow or Choke Type restricts the flow rate during surges, minimizing potential damage. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing system reliability and longevity by mitigating the risks associated with pressure variations in various industries.</p></p>
