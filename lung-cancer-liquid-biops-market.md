<p><strong>The Lung Cancer Liquid Biops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The lung cancer liquid biopsy market comprises various technologies used to detect cancer-related information from bodily fluids. Exosomes and RNA focus on small vesicles that carry genetic material and proteins, providing insights into tumor characteristics. Circulating tumor cells (CTCs) involve isolating intact cancer cells from blood samples, allowing for detailed analysis of the tumor's biological features. Circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) examines fragments of DNA shed into the bloodstream, offering non-invasive means to identify genetic mutations and monitor treatment response.</p></p>
