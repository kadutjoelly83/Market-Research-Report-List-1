<p><p>The atmospheric pressure sensor market is divided into two main types: analog output and digital output sensors. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals that vary proportionally with pressure changes, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, digital output sensors convert pressure readings into a digital format, allowing for more precise data transmission and easier integration with modern electronic systems. Both types serve diverse industries, including meteorology, aviation, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
