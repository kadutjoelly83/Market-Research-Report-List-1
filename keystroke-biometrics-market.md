<p><strong>The Keystroke Biometrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Keystroke Biometric</li><li>Dynamic Keystroke Biometrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Keystroke biometrics consists of two main market types: static and dynamic. Static keystroke biometrics captures the unique typing patterns of a user based on their input of fixed text, typically during an enrollment phase. This method focuses on timing data from a specific phrase. In contrast, dynamic keystroke biometrics analyzes real-time typing behavior, including rhythm and pressure, to authenticate users continuously as they type. Both methods enhance security by using individual typing habits as a biometric identification mechanism.</p></p>
