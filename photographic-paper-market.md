<p><ul><li>Silver Halide Photographic Paper</li><li>Inkjet Photographic Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photographic paper market is primarily divided into two types: silver halide photographic paper and inkjet photographic paper. Silver halide photographic paper relies on chemical processes to produce images, typically used in traditional film photography and professional printing. In contrast, inkjet photographic paper is designed for use with inkjet printers, allowing for digital image printing and offering a variety of finishes. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from professional photographers to hobbyists, influencing preferences based on image quality and printing technology.</p></p>
