<p><p>Combination starters are essential components in motor control, classified into different market types based on functionality. Full-voltage non-reversing starters provide direct power to motors without allowing reverse operation. Full-voltage reversing starters enable the user to reverse motor direction. Multi-speed starters cater to applications requiring variable speeds, adjusting power delivery accordingly. Reduced voltage starters minimize inrush current, protecting motors during startup while controlling torque and speed. These types serve diverse industrial needs, ensuring efficient motor operation across various applications.</p></p>
