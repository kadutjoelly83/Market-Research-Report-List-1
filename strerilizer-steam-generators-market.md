<p><p>The sterilizer steam generators market is divided into two main types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are built directly into the sterilization system, providing a compact and efficient solution, ideal for smaller facilities. In contrast, remote boilers operate separately from the sterilizer, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability, making them suitable for larger operations or multiple units. Both types serve essential roles in delivering steam for sterilization processes across healthcare, pharmaceutical, and laboratory sectors.</p></p>
