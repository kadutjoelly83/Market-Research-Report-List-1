<p><p>Point-of-care (POC) molecular diagnostic solutions are advanced technologies that enable rapid detection and analysis of pathogens, genetic conditions, or biomarkers directly at the site of patient care. Recent trends also include the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance diagnostic accuracy, alongside growing investments in research and development.
