<p><p>The food grade silicone mold release market is categorized into two main types: silicone internal release agents and silicone external release agents. Silicone internal release agents are blended directly into the silicone materials used for molding, providing a built-in barrier that facilitates easy part release. In contrast, silicone external release agents are applied as a coating on the surface of molds prior to casting, ensuring a smooth separation of the finished product from the mold while maintaining food safety standards.</p></p>
