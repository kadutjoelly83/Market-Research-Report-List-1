Middle-temperature unleaded solder paste offers a balance between workability and thermal performance, suitable for standard soldering tasks. High-temperature unleaded solder paste withstands elevated soldering temperatures, ideal for high-stress applications like automotive and aerospace electronics, ensuring reliable connections in demanding environments.</p></p>
