<p><strong>The Silicon Dioxide Sputtering Target Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotatable Transformation</li><li>Non Rotating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon dioxide sputtering target market is divided into two main types: rotatable transformation and non-rotating type. Rotatable transformation targets enable continuous deposition and improved utilization of material by rotating during the sputtering process, enhancing film uniformity and efficiency. In contrast, non-rotating targets involve stationary deposition, often leading to lower material efficiency and potential non-uniformity in film thickness. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, desired film characteristics, and production efficiency.</p></p>
