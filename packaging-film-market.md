<p><p>The packaging film market encompasses various types of films suited for different applications. PP (Polypropylene) films are known for their durability and versatility, making them ideal for food packaging. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) films offer high clarity and strength, widely used in electronics and lamination. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are lightweight and moisture-resistant, suitable for labels and packaging. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) films provide good sealability and transparency. Other films include polyethylenes and biodegradable options, catering to distinct packaging needs.</p></p>
