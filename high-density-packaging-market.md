<p><p>The high-density packaging market includes several advanced technologies designed to enhance the performance and miniaturization of electronic components. MCM (Multi-Chip Module) packaging integrates multiple chips into a single module for improved efficiency. MCP (Multi-Chip Package) combines various chip technologies into one package, optimizing space. SIP (System in Package) consolidates different functions within a single package, enhancing functionality. 3D TSV (Through-Silicon Via) packaging connects multiple silicon layers vertically, increasing density and performance while reducing interconnect lengths, leading to faster speeds.</p></p>
