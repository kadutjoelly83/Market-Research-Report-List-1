<p><p>Solderless breadboards are essential tools for prototyping electronic circuits. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Assembly (On Frame)**: Includes breadboards integrated into a frame for stability and ease of use in assembling complex circuits. </p><p>2. **Terminal and Distribution Strips (No Frame)**: These are standalone strips without a frame, facilitating the connection of various components without the need for structural support. </p><p>3. **Powered (On Frame)**: These breadboards come with built-in power supply options, offering convenience for powering circuits directly within the frame setup.</p></p>
