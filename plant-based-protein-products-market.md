<p><p>The plant-based protein products market is primarily divided into two types: complete and incomplete plant proteins. Complete plant proteins contain all nine essential amino acids, making them nutritionally comprehensive and ideal for muscle building and overall health. Examples include quinoa and soy. In contrast, incomplete plant proteins lack one or more essential amino acids and typically come from sources like beans and grains. Consumers often combine incomplete proteins to achieve a balanced amino acid profile, enhancing their nutritional value.</p></p>
