<p><ul><li>Sliding Bearing</li><li>Rolling Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The main bearing market is primarily categorized into sliding bearings and rolling bearings. Sliding bearings operate with minimal friction and are suitable for applications requiring high load capacities and low speeds, utilizing a sliding motion between surfaces. In contrast, rolling bearings use balls or rollers to reduce friction and are ideal for high-speed applications by distributing the load over a larger area. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing their design, material choice, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
