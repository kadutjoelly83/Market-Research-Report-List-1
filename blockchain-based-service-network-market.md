<p><p>The Blockchain-based Service Network (BSN) encompasses two main market types: permissionless and permissioned blockchains. Permissionless blockchains allow any user to participate and validate transactions, promoting decentralization and openness, exemplified by networks like Bitcoin and Ethereum. In contrast, permissioned blockchains restrict access to selected participants, enhancing privacy and security for enterprises with specific governance needs. Both markets cater to different applications, with permissionless frameworks favoring innovation and permissioned networks prioritizing compliance and control in business environments.</p></p>
