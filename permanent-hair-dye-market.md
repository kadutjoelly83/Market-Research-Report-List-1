<p><ul><li>PPDA Based Hair Dye</li><li>PTD Based Hair Dye</li><li>Other Types Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permanent hair dye market primarily includes three types: PPDA-based hair dyes, PTD-based hair dyes, and other types. PPDA-based dyes contain para-phenylenediamine, known for its long-lasting color, though it may cause allergic reactions. PTD-based dyes utilize para-toluene diamine, offering a gentler alternative with reduced allergic potential. Other types encompass various formulations, including natural and organic dyes, catering to diverse consumer preferences for safety and environmental concerns. Each type targets distinct market segments with unique formulations and benefits.</p></p>
